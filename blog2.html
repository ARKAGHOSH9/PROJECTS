What is CSS?

August 1st,2024

CSS (Cascading Style Sheets) is a language used to style and layout web pages.\n It controls the visual appearance of HTML elements, such as their colors, fonts, spacing, and positioning, allowing you to create attractive and consistent designs across a website.\n CSS separates content (HTML) from design, making it easier to maintain and update the look of a site.
